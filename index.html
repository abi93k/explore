Explore

Explore for Pebble lets you browse nearby venues on your watch. Explore provides you with detailed information about venues like rating,distance from your current location and address of the venue. You can also forward venue directions to your Android Phone from your Pebble Watch. (Requires the Android Companion App to be installed). The application uses the Foursquare API.
